Pear Learning Sessions

What it is?

Peer learning sessions are weekly intraclub activities based on the idea of collective learning and healthy discussions. The motive behind is to not only enhance general and special interests in environmental issues but to learn and apply the cutting edge technologies(like bio-toilets,plastic recycling etc.) used around the world.This also inculcates cooperative discipline in club members.
